# Rice n' Greens Casserole

A simple, hearty Southern-style casserole loaded with rice, mustard greens, sausage, corn, tomatoes, and a little jalapeño kick.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup uncooked rice
- 1¾ cups water
- 1 (27 oz) can mustard greens, drained
- 1 regular can sweet corn, drained
- 1 regular can diced tomatoes (with juices)
- ½ cup sliced jalapeños, drained
- 1 cup sliced sausage

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **325°F (163°C)**.
2. In a **5-quart casserole dish**, combine the rice, water, and diced tomatoes.
3. Drain the mustard greens, corn, and jalapeños.
4. Add the greens, corn, jalapeños, and sliced sausage to the casserole dish.
5. Stir gently until everything is evenly distributed.
6. Cover with a lid and bake for **1 hour**.
7. Remove from the oven and stir gently. The rice will likely appear undercooked at this stage—this is normal.
8. Cover again and return to the oven for **1 additional hour**.
9. Remove from the oven and let rest for a few minutes before serving.

## Notes

- Place a **cookie sheet or baking sheet** under the casserole dish while baking. The mixture may bubble over slightly during the second hour.
- For extra flavor, use smoked sausage or andouille sausage.
- Adjust the amount of jalapeños to suit your preferred heat level.

**Yield:** Approximately 6–8 servings
