# Baked Rice Dinner

> **Takeaway:** Creamy baked rice, savory soups, and your choice of meat come together in a simple one-pan meal that's hearty, comforting, and easy to customize.

## Overview

This is one of those classic dump-and-bake dinners where everything cooks together in a single baking dish.

The combination of **Golden Mushroom Soup** and **French Onion Soup** creates a rich, savory sauce that flavors the rice as it cooks. Add sausage, pork chops, chicken, or your favorite protein and let the oven do the work.

Perfect for busy weeknights when you want a filling meal with minimal effort and cleanup.

---

## Ingredients

### Rice Base

- 1 can Campbell's Golden Mushroom Soup
- 1 can Campbell's French Onion Soup
- 1 1/3 cups water
- 2 cups uncooked rice

### Protein Options

Choose one:

- Smoked sausage links
  - Zummo's Party Time Links work especially well
- Pork chops
- Chicken breasts
- Chicken thighs
- Other sausage varieties

---

## Instructions

### 1. Prepare the Sauce

In a mixing bowl combine:

- 1 can Golden Mushroom Soup
- 1 can French Onion Soup
- 1 cup of Water
```
Note all together this should be close to 3 cups of liquid.
If it's not enought the rice will not come out right.
If it's to much the rice will be soggy and mushy and not right.
```

Whisk or stir until well blended.

### 2. Build the Rice Layer

Pour the soup mixture into a large baking dish.

Add:

```text
2 cups uncooked rice
```

Stir until the rice is evenly distributed throughout the liquid.

### 3. Add the Meat

Arrange your chosen protein on top of the rice mixture.

Examples:

- Whole sausage links
- Pork chops
- Chicken breasts
- Chicken thighs

Leave the meat on top so it can slowly flavor the rice as everything bakes together.

### 4. Cover and Bake

Cover the baking dish tightly with aluminum foil.

Bake at:

```text
350°F (177°C)
```

for:

```text
45 minutes to an hour.
```
Pairs well with some green beans cooked on the stove.
